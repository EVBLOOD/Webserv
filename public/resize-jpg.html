Jpg/jpeg resizer tool What is a jpg/jpeg resizer
This online tool resizes JPG, JPEG, and JPEG2000 graphics files. It lets you set the new output picture size (width x height) and keep the aspect ratio. In the options, you'll find the width and height fields where you can enter the new dimensions of the picture. When you open your JPG/JPEG for editing, these fields will be pre-filled with the dimensions of your original image. You can add size to each dimension to get a larger or a smaller JPG/JPEG. You can also easily upsize or downsize your JPG and JPEG pictures interactively by dragging them by the resize bars that are located in the corners of the picture. Jpgabulous!
